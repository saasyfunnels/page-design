Questions

Frequently Asked

What does "unlimited done-for-you" actually mean on Smart & SaaSy? +
It means you can submit as many tasks as you need — no monthly cap, no overage fees. Small tasks like blog publishing, newsletter sending, and social scheduling go into a rolling queue and are completed as submitted. Larger builds like workflows, pages, and automations have a 21-day standard turnaround. Annual members get priority queue status, which significantly reduces that wait time.
What counts as a "small task" vs a "large build"? +
Small tasks are execution-only — publishing a blog post, sending a newsletter, scheduling social posts, or making a copy edit. Large builds require our team to actually construct something from scratch: a new funnel page, an automation workflow, an email sequence setup. Not sure which yours is? Just ask when you submit and we'll let you know upfront.
Can I upgrade from Essentials to Smart & SaaSy later? +
Absolutely. All your contacts, automations, pages, and courses carry over when you upgrade. You're unlocking more of the platform and adding the done-for-you layer — nothing gets reset. Most clients start on Essentials to get familiar, then upgrade when they're ready to hand off the heavy lifting.
Do I need to be tech-savvy? +
You don't need to be technical, but you will need time to learn a new platform if you're on Essentials and self-managing. If you'd prefer to stay hands-off, Smart & SaaSy is designed exactly for that — you describe what you need, we build it. You can always change plans as your comfort level grows.
What's included in the Kajabi migration? +
We migrate your website (up to 10 pages), up to 4 funnels, up to 5 course or membership products, 1 community, unlimited contacts, up to 10 email sequences, and up to 20 blog articles. Migration takes approximately 2 weeks. It's free for Smart & SaaSy annual members, or available as a paid add-on ($1,000 USD full / from $500 USD mini) on all other plans.
Is there a setup fee or lock-in contract? +
SaaSy Essentials has a one-time $149 setup fee. Smart & SaaSy has no setup fee. Neither plan has a lock-in contract on the monthly option. Annual plans are paid upfront and come with priority queue, free migration, and 2 months free — but there's no lock-in beyond that commitment.

GoHighLevel is the most powerful all-in-one business platform available for coaches and service providers. Rather than building a platform from scratch, SaaSy Funnels is built on top of GHL — which means you get the full power of an enterprise-grade system, wrapped in our templates, onboarding support, and done-for-you implementation team.
How does the annual Smart & SaaSy plan work? +
Annual Smart & SaaSy is $4,970/year — that's 2 months free compared to paying monthly.
