Questions

Frequently Asked

What does "unlimited done-for-you" actually mean on Smart & SaaSy? +
It means you can submit as many tasks as you need — no monthly cap, no overage fees. Small tasks like blog publishing, newsletter sending, and social scheduling go into a rolling queue. Larger builds like workflows, pages, and automations have a 21-day standard turnaround. Annual members get priority queue status, which significantly reduces that wait time.
What counts as a "small task" vs a "large build"? +
Small tasks are execution-only — publishing a blog post, sending a newsletter, scheduling social posts, or making a copy edit. Large builds require our team to construct something: a new funnel page, an automation workflow, an email sequence setup. Not sure which yours is? Just ask when you submit.
Can I upgrade from Essentials to Smart & SaaSy later? +
Absolutely. All your contacts, automations, pages, and courses carry over when you upgrade. You're just unlocking more of the platform and adding the done-for-you layer on top. Nothing gets reset. Most clients start on Essentials to get familiar, then upgrade when ready.
Do I need to be tech-savvy? +
You don't need to be technical, but you will need time to learn a new platform if you're on Essentials and self-managing. If you'd prefer to stay hands-off, Smart & SaaSy is designed exactly for that — you describe what you need, we build it.
What's included in the Kajabi migration? +
We migrate your website (up to 10 pages), up to 4 funnels, up to 5 course or membership products, 1 community, unlimited contacts, up to 10 email sequences, and up to 20 blog articles. Migration takes approximately 2 weeks. Free for Smart & SaaSy annual members, or available as a paid add-on ($1,000 USD full / from $500 USD mini).
Is there a setup fee or lock-in contract? +
SaaSy Essentials has a one-time $149 setup fee. Smart & SaaSy has no setup fee. Neither plan has a lock-in contract on the monthly option. Annual plans are paid upfront and come with priority queue, free migration, and 2 months free.

GoHighLevel is the most powerful all-in-one business platform available for coaches and service providers. SaaSy Funnels is built on top of GHL — giving you the full power of an enterprise-grade system, wrapped in our templates, onboarding support, and done-for-you implementation team.
